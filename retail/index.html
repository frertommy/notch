How Quick mode works

Pick a team. We do the rest.
1
Pick an outcome
Tap a green, amber, or red button on any game. That's your pick.
2
The % is your payout
If you're right, you win that % on top of your stake. Bigger % = bigger reward (and bigger risk).
$50 on Arsenal +92% → win $46 if Arsenal wins. Lose $50 if not.
3
Auto-closes at game end
No managing positions. We close it the moment the game ends and pay out automatically.
4
Stack for bigger payouts
Combos multiply. Pick 2 or 3 games, all have to be right, but payouts compound.
3-game favorites stack → +501% combined
5
Leverage up top
Use the 20× / 30× / 50× / 100× pills to dial payouts up or down. Same risk shape — just bigger numbers.
Want to hold positions past the game? Set your own size and leverage?
